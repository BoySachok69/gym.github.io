What makes us stand out from other gyms?

Spacious

All our gyms boast at least 500 square feet of space. This is to ensure that our patrons have adequate breathing space to work out and proper space to lay their yoga mats to do their own workouts

Equipment

We only use high-end equipment that are imported from Germany. All our machines are tested rigorously and chances of failure is zero. We have a wide range of dumbbells and weights ranging from 1kg up to 50kg

Convenience

Our gyms are located in the heartlands and are near to MRT stations and bus stops.

Cheap

As compared to other world-class gyms, ours are cheaper in terms of memberships and also per entry.
